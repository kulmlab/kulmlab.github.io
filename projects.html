Featured Projects

Our flagship research initiatives

Ongoing

Efficient Large Language Models

Developing methods to reduce the computational cost of training and deploying large language models while maintaining their capabilities. Our research includes model compression, efficient attention mechanisms, and hardware-aware optimization.

GitHub Papers Demo
Ongoing

Knowledge-Grounded Dialogue

Building conversational AI systems that can access and reason over external knowledge bases to provide accurate, informative, and engaging responses. We focus on retrieval-augmented generation and fact verification.

GitHub Papers Demo
Ongoing

Multilingual Language Understanding

Extending NLP capabilities to low-resource languages through cross-lingual transfer learning. Our goal is to make language technology accessible to speakers of all languages, not just English.

GitHub Papers Dataset
Ongoing

AI Safety & Alignment

Researching methods to ensure language models are safe, unbiased, and aligned with human values. We develop techniques for detecting and mitigating harmful outputs and improving model transparency.

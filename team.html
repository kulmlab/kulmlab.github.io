Professor

Principal investigator leading our research

Jaehyung Seo

Assistant Professor, KonKuk University

Affiliation: Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence)

Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering, Korea University (Seoul)

M.S. in Computer Science and Engineering, Korea University (Seoul)

B.S. in English Language and Business School at Korea University (Seoul)

Research Interests

Natural Language Processing, Commonsense Reasoning, Hallucination, Model Editing, Multimodal Understanding

Research Highlights & Collaboration

2025: Published 9 papers at top-tier international CS/AI conferences, including ICLR, ACL, EMNLP, and NAACL.

2024: Published 7 papers at leading CS/AI conferences, including ACL, EMNLP, EACL, and COLING.

2023: Published 5 papers at premier CS/AI conferences, including ACL, EMNLP, and IJCNLP.

Over the past five years, published +15 papers in Q1–Q2 Computer Science journals, including KBS, ESWA, IEEE, Applied Sciences, Mathematics.

Collaborations:
• Industry–academia collaborations with Samsung Research, Samsung Fire & Marine Insurance, Samsung SDS, KT, Hyundai Motor Company, NCAI, and NAVER;
• International research collaborations with A*STAR (Singapore) and York University (Canada).

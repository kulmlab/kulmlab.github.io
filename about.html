About Our Lab

Pioneering research in Language Intelligence & Representation

Our Mission

ELION stands for Elucidating Language Intelligence & RepresentatiON.

Our mission is to elucidate how language models represent knowledge and reason, enabling interpretable, controllable, and trustworthy language intelligence that can ultimately interact with the real world.

Ultimately, we aim to extend language intelligence beyond text, enabling models to infer physical and social commonsense from non-linguistic signals and to interact with the real world. By doing so, ELION Lab strives to provide the core technologies and recipes for next-generation language intelligence that can reason, adapt, and operate in real-world environments.
